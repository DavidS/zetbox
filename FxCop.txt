Important!!!

If you Edit FxCop.fxcop with the FxCop UI then you have to fix the AssemblyReferenceDirectories:

 <Targets>
  <AssemblyReferenceDirectories>
    <Directory>$(ProjectDir)/bin/Debug/bin/Server/</Directory>
    <Directory>$(ProjectDir)/bin/Debug/bin/Server/Fallback/</Directory>
    <Directory>$(ProjectDir)/Tracing/</Directory>
   <Directory>$(ProjectDir)/WPFLibrary/obj/Debug/</Directory>
  </AssemblyReferenceDirectories>
